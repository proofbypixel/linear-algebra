Visualizing Basis Vectors in Business Operations
18 December, 2025 | By Abhinav

I've just uploaded to the repo my latest visualization on Linear Independence. This project resolves the intuition gap between abstract vector math and real-world resource allocation.

Specifically, we look at the question of bounding the "Efficiency Frontier" when your resources (basis vectors) are imperfect.

1. Vector Space & Span

A vector space $V$ over a field $\mathbb{F}$ is a set where vector addition and scalar multiplication are defined and satisfy specific axioms.

$$ \text{span}(\vec{v}_1, \dots, \vec{v}_n) = \{ \sum_{i=1}^{n} \alpha_i \vec{v}_i \mid \alpha_i \in \mathbb{F} \} $$
